Coffee Reviews

Maglianero's

Best coffee in Vermont so far. I had the hazelnut iced latte on a hot day and it was simply delicious. Coconut macaroon was also delectable. Maglianero offers a wide range of pastries, coffees and teas. I also discovered an amazing sparkling water, Saratoga.
Laid-back ambiance, modern / loft architecture and good music are just perfect. You can even park your bike inside the coffee shop.

Muddy Water's

It's the atmosphere of Muddy Waters that really makes this coffee shop unique. If walking into a warm, wood cabin makes you feel good, you will undoubtedly love the aura of this place.

Have a craving for quality coffee or fruit/vegetable juices? Look no further: Muddy Waters has it. They even have beer on tap. That's pretty awesome.

The staff members are friendly and down-to-earth. You won't likely find an unkind person working here.

August First Bakery & Cafe's

Beautiful cafe with friendly and patient service. The exposed brick and garage type doors add to the comfortable atmosphere. I had a delicious cup of espresso.
